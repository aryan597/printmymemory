## Tips for Best Results

1. **Use --ar (aspect ratio)** that matches your website layout
2. **Add "photorealistic, 4k, product photography"** for best quality
3. **Use dark backgrounds** to match your website theme (#0a0e1a)
4. **Include "orange accent lighting"** for brand consistency
5. **Generate multiple variations** and pick the best
